Who I Am

About Me

A thoughtful creator building his future — one experience at a time.

I'm Bhaskar Gupta, an B.Tech student from BIT Sindri,Dhanbad who fell in love with the space where technology meets human emotion. I don't just write code — I think about how it makes people feel. Every button, every transition, every word on screen is a conversation with the user.

My curiosity started with building simple web pages and evolved into a deep fascination with UI/UX design, user psychology, and microcopy. I'm drawn to interfaces that feel effortless — the kind where you don't notice the design because it just works.

Beyond screens, I sharpen my strategic thinking through chess, explore creative writing to understand storytelling, and constantly participate in competitions and hackathons to push my boundaries. I believe growth happens in the quiet moments — between the lines of code you refactor at 2 AM and the designs you iterate for the fifth time.
